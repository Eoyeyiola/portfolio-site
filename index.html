Credit Risk Classification

SMOTE & Machine Learning

Many lending institutions struggle with classifying loan statuses — especially when data is imbalanced. In this project, I applied SMOTE to oversample underrepresented classes, trained models like Logistic Regression and Random Forest, and visualized results with Power BI. The outcome? A more balanced classification model and actionable risk insights.
