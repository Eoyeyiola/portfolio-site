About Me

Hi, I'm Ema.

I'm a data analyst with a background in entrepreneurship and a passion for transforming messy data into meaningful stories. My journey started when I ran a small business in Lagos, Nigeria — and learned firsthand how powerful data can be in driving better decisions.

Today, I work at the intersection of business and analytics — cleaning data, building dashboards, and designing models that answer real questions. I love using tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau to bring ideas to life.

Whether it's uncovering customer insights, optimizing operations, or visualizing complex patterns, I thrive on making data accessible and actionable for everyone — from startups to enterprise teams.
